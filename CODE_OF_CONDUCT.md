# code of conduct

## our pledge

we are committed to making participation in for the open dev a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## expected behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- welcome newcomers
- focus on what is best for the community
- accept constructive criticism gracefully

## unacceptable behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- trolling or inflammatory comments
- publishing others' private information
- other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## enforcement

report issues to hello@fortheopen.dev. maintainers may remove contributions or ban participants who violate this code.

adapted from the [contributor covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
